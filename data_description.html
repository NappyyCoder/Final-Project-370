About the Dataset

This comprehensive dataset contains information about video game sales across different regions, spanning multiple decades of the gaming industry. The data includes details about game titles, platforms, publishers, genres, and sales figures from various geographical regions.

Data Structure

  • Name: Title of the video game
  • Platform: Gaming platform (e.g., PS4, Xbox, PC)
  • Year: Release year of the game
  • Genre: Category of the game
  • Publisher: Company that published the game
  • Sales Data:
    • NA_Sales: Sales in North America (in millions)
    • EU_Sales: Sales in Europe (in millions)
    • JP_Sales: Sales in Japan (in millions)
    • Other_Sales: Sales in other regions (in millions)
    • Global_Sales: Total worldwide sales (in millions)

Data Coverage

The dataset covers video game sales from the 1980s to the present, providing a comprehensive view of the gaming industry's evolution. It includes major gaming platforms and represents sales data from key markets worldwide.

Data Sources

The sales data has been compiled from various reliable sources including industry reports, market research firms, and official sales records. This ensures a high level of accuracy and reliability in the analysis.
